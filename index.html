STRAND RECOMMENDER SYSTEM FOR SAN NICOLAS PANGASINAN RED ARROW SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL


Stepping in a new milestone as a SHS student is about more than just meeting new people and learning about a subject. More importantly, it involves imagining and planning your ideal job and career path.
Our System goal is to help the school in monitoring their grade 10 students to be wise and give assistance in decision making for their career in senior high school in accordance with their knowledge and skills. Also, providing students about giving each strand clear and more transparent informations.

Strand Recommender System

The Strand Recommender System for San Nicolas Pangasinan Red Arrow Senior High School aimed and intended to use of the grade 10 students before their school year ends.

This project aims to help grade 10 students of Red Arrow High School to choose strand who are having difficulty and struggling selecting/choosing strand that appropriate for them to enroll, through the use of taking the general assessment in the proposed system.

The Strand Recommender System for San Nicolas Pangasinan Red Arrow Senior High School will provide a general assessment. The general assessment is mandatory to take in the grade 10 level of the school. The assessment result will be used as basis in order to make strand recommendations for the grade 10 students.

What is Recommender System?

The system will also help the school guidance counselor in evaluating and giving assistance in selecting strand to the school grade 10 level. This feature will help the guidance councelor work more easier wherein the students needed to answer guidance counselor’s questions in the system.

The proposed web-based system will also have the feature to disseminate more detailed and transparent information about the strands students may want to take in moving up Senior High School. Moreover, the system will also provide a room called community forum wherein the grade 10 students of the Red Arrow High School can be able to express their thoughts, opinion and feedback from the school.

How can the Strand Recommender helps?

This research intends to assist all the faculty and teachers of Red Arrow High School in making their work more efficient in terms of report generation by utilizing a system that allows them to monitor the student progress after they take the general assessment that can be viewed in the database. The technology will provide the organization with numerous benefits, including a better, stronger, and more efficient system in assisting grade 10 students in choosing the strand suited for them.

The developers endorsed their proposal which will help the school institution implement a wonderful and precise system. The designers did their best to fulfill the current needs of the trade by making a computerized strand recommender system that will offer assistance to their grade 10 students.

RED ARROW HIGH SCHOOL

The Red Arrow High School is a private high school located at Magsaysay Street, in the municipality of San Nicolas, Pangasinan. The school accepts and teaches Junior High School and Senior High School students. The school is founded in the year 1946 and still operating up into these days.

The pioneers conceived the idea of establishing a High School named after the famous Red Arrow Division of the 32nd Infantry Division of the US Army. This unit was responsible in clearing the Caraballo Mountains of the elements of the Japanese Imperial Army in 1945.

Today, RED ARROW HIGH SCHOOL continues to give affordable and quality education that is relevant and accessible to its students. It reaffirms its commitments to serve the growing needs of the youth of San Nicolas, Pangasinan and its surroundings communities.
